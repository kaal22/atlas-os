# Fractions — sharing fairly

A fraction names equal parts of a whole. The bottom number (denominator) is how
many equal parts; the top number (numerator) is how many parts you have.

## Try it

1. Cut an apple into **4** equal pieces. Each piece is **1/4**.
2. Eat **3** pieces. You ate **3/4** of the apple.
3. Two halves make a whole: **1/2 + 1/2 = 1**.

## Check

- Which is larger: **1/3** or **1/5**? (Hint: fewer equal pieces means each piece is bigger.)
- Draw a pizza with 8 slices and shade **5/8**.
