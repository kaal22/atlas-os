# Simple machines at home

Simple machines help us do work with less effort. Look around the house:

| Machine | Example | What it does |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| Lever | See-saw, crowbar, bottle opener | Multiplies force |
| Wheel & axle | Door knob, rolling suitcase | Reduces friction |
| Inclined plane | Ramp, wedge | Spreads work over distance |
| Pulley | Curtain cord, flagpole | Changes direction of pull |
| Screw | Jar lid, wood screw | Inclined plane wrapped around |

## Challenge

Find three simple machines in your kitchen and name which type each one is.
