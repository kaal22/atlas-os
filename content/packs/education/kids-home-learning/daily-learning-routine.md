# Daily home-learning routine

Atlas home-learning starter. Licence: CC BY 4.0.

A short, steady routine beats long irregular sessions.

## Suggested day (about 60–90 minutes total)
1. **Warm-up (5 min)** — count aloud, skip-count, or a quick stretch.
2. **Maths (15–20 min)** — one lesson section + practice questions.
3. **Reading (15–20 min)** — read aloud or independently; talk about the story.
4. **Science or geography (15–20 min)** — one activity or observation.
5. **Review (5 min)** — what did we learn? What was tricky?

## Tips for adults / tutors
- Sit beside the learner; celebrate effort, not only correct answers.
- Stop while energy is still good — end on success.
- Use household objects as counters, rulers, and science tools.
- Ask Atlas Chat / local tutor agents to quiz from these lesson files offline.

## Weekly goals example
- Finish one maths topic and one reading challenge.
- Complete one science try-it and one geography map activity.
- Log three new vocabulary words.
