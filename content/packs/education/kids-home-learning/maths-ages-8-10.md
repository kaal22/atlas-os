## Place value and rounding
- Thousands, hundreds, tens, ones.
- Round to the nearest 10 or 100 (look at the digit to the right; 5 or more rounds up).
- Compare and order numbers up to 10,000.

## Multiplication and division
- Know times tables for 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, and 10.
- Division as sharing and as grouping.
- Remainders: 17 ÷ 5 = 3 remainder 2.
- Multiply a 2-digit number by a 1-digit number using expanded form (23 × 4 = 20×4 + 3×4).

## Fractions
- Halves, thirds, quarters, fifths.
- Equivalent fractions: 1/2 = 2/4 = 3/6.
- Find a fraction of a quantity: 1/4 of 20 = 5.

## Perimeter and area
- Perimeter is the distance around a shape (add the side lengths).
- Area of a rectangle = length × width (square units).

## Practice questions
1. Round 347 to the nearest 10.
2. What is 7 × 8?
3. What is 1/3 of 24?
4. Perimeter of a 5 cm by 3 cm rectangle?
5. 46 ÷ 5 = ? remainder ?

## Answers
1. 350  
2. 56  
3. 8  
4. 16 cm  
5. 9 remainder 1
