# Reading together (ages 6–8)

Atlas home-learning starter. Licence: CC BY 4.0.

## Daily habit
- Read aloud for 10–15 minutes.
- Ask: Who is in the story? Where are they? What happened first, next, last?
- Point to new words and sound them out slowly.
- Re-read favourite pages — fluency grows with practice.

## Phonics reminders
- Blend sounds: c-a-t → cat.
- Digraphs: sh, ch, th, ng, wh.
- Split digraphs (magic e): cake, bike, note.
- Sight words to practise: the, said, you, are, they, because, people, could.

## Vocabulary
- When you meet a new word, guess from the sentence, then check.
- Keep a “word jar” of interesting words and reuse one each day in speech.

## Story talk prompts
- How do you think the character feels? What clues tell you that?
- What might happen next?
- Would you make the same choice? Why?
- Retell the story in three sentences.

## Mini reading challenge
Pick a short picture book. After reading, draw the setting and label three things
you saw in the text (not only in the pictures).
