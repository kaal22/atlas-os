# Reading deeper (ages 8–10)

Atlas home-learning starter. Licence: CC BY 4.0.

## Independent reading
- Aim for 20 minutes of quiet reading most days.
- Mix fiction and non-fiction (science, history, how-to).
- Keep a simple reading log: title, pages, one sentence about the main idea.

## Comprehension skills
- **Retrieve**: find a fact that is written in the text.
- **Infer**: use clues plus what you know (the author does not always say it outright).
- **Summarise**: say the most important points in your own words.
- **Vocabulary in context**: replace a hard word with a simpler synonym and check if the sentence still works.

## Non-fiction features
Contents, headings, captions, diagrams, glossary, index — practise using each.

## Discussion questions
1. What is the author’s purpose (entertain, inform, persuade)?
2. Which evidence supports the main claim?
3. How would the story change if it were set somewhere else?

## Writing link
After reading a chapter, write five sentences: beginning, problem, attempt, turning
point, ending. Use at least two new words from the text.
