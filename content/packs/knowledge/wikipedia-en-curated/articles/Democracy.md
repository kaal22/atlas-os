# Democracy

Democracy is a system of government in which power is vested in the people, who rule
either directly or through freely elected representatives.

## Features
Common features include free and fair elections, protection of civil liberties, rule of law,
and accountable institutions. Forms include direct democracy and representative democracy.

## History
The word comes from Greek *dēmokratia* ("rule of the people"). Classical Athens is an early
example; modern democracies vary widely in constitution and practice.
