# Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantity, structure, space, and change. It uses
abstraction and logical reasoning to form general results (theorems) from defined assumptions.

## Major areas
- **Arithmetic and number theory** — operations and properties of numbers.
- **Algebra** — symbols and equations.
- **Geometry** — shapes, space, and measurement.
- **Calculus** — rates of change and accumulation.
- **Statistics and probability** — data and uncertainty.

## Applications
Mathematics underpins science, engineering, computing, finance, and everyday problem-solving
from measuring ingredients to navigating with maps.
