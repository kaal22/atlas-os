# Solar System

The Solar System comprises the Sun and the objects bound to it by gravity, including eight
planets, dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

## Planets
- **Terrestrial:** Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars (rocky).
- **Gas giants:** Jupiter, Saturn.
- **Ice giants:** Uranus, Neptune.

Earth’s Moon is a natural satellite. The asteroid belt lies mainly between Mars and Jupiter.
Beyond Neptune lie the Kuiper belt and distant small bodies such as comets.

## Formation
The Solar System formed about 4.6 billion years ago from a collapsing molecular cloud; the
Sun ignited at the centre and a protoplanetary disk formed the planets.
