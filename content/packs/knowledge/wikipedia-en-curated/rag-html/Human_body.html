Human body

The human body is the structure of a human being. It is composed of many systems that work

together to sustain life.

Major systems

- **Skeletal** — support and protection.

- **Muscular** — movement.

- **Circulatory** — heart and blood vessels transport oxygen and nutrients.

- **Respiratory** — lungs exchange gases.

- **Digestive** — breaks down food for energy and materials.

- **Nervous** — brain, spinal cord, and nerves coordinate responses.

- **Immune** — defends against pathogens.

Cells and organs

Tissues form organs; organs form systems. Homeostasis keeps internal conditions (such as

temperature and blood chemistry) within healthy ranges.
